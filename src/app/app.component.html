About

It was in 2014 that I decided to create my website project to showcase a journalist friend. After five years of study and more than eight years of work, my passion remains intact, and I'm still fascinated by the speed at which my profession is evolving.

What motivates me today is working with motivated people to simplify complex information systems and architectures as much as possible. I love beautiful code as much as I hate technical debt. Despite these years of experience, I'm still learning every day through my work, but above all thanks to all the great developers I've been lucky enough to work with!

Apart from web development, I'm passionate about surfing, which has led me to travel a lot to beautiful countries. I also enjoy (badly) playing guitar and piano from time to time.
